# Spec — thresholds, one line of why each

Terse companion to `principles.md`. The exact numbers, grouped by funnel stage. Persuasion lives in `principles.md`; this is the lookup sheet. No sizing, stop, or R:R numbers exist here (out of scope by design).

---

## Regime

- **>90% of superperformers launch out of a correction / bear bottom** — regime is a first-order base rate; almost none begin mid-bear.
- **Funds rarely raise >5–10% cash even in dreadful conditions** — they are structurally ~90%+ invested, so they must lose in bears; individual cash is structural alpha.
- **Leadership concentrates in ~3-4 up to 8-10 groups** — count names hitting 52-week highs early; concentrate in the top 4-5 sectors.
- **Lockout-rally index pullbacks ~3–5%** — off an important bottom the first leg refuses to give the awaited pullback; that refusal is the buy tell.
- **Regime read (corrected):** new-high vs new-low spread + its significant EXPANSION + leadership behavior (list expanding/buckling) + your own stop-out rate. **NOT** a follow-through-day or distribution-day count — that mechanism is absent from the method.

## Trend Template (hard gate — all 8 or reject)

1. Price > 150-day AND 200-day MA — institutions buy above the trend proxies.
2. 150-day MA > 200-day MA — intermediate trend leads the long trend.
3. 200-day MA trending up ≥1 month (pref. 4–5 months) — the long trend must have actually turned.
4. 50-day MA > 150-day AND 200-day MA — short trend leads both.
5. Price > 50-day MA — currently riding the short trend.
6. Price ≥30% above its 52-week low — confirms a real advance off the bottom, not a dead-cat level.
7. Price within 25% of its 52-week high — near the line of least resistance, not buried under overhead.
8. RS rank ≥70 (pref. 80s–90s) — only outperformers; relative strength is the scarcity.

- **Base rates: 99% above the 200-day / 96% above the 50-day before the move** — being below either bets on a <1% tail.
- **Stage-2 confirmation: prior 25–30% rally off the 52-week low, often far more** (Amgen +80%) — the surrendered first leg is the price of confirmation.
- **~95% of Trend-Template survivors die at the fundamental/RS/volatility screen** — the chart is the easy qualifier; fundamentals × RS is where it narrows.
- **Intra-stage-2 bases: 5–26 weeks common** (broader waves 4–5wk to a year+) — disambiguates a buyable pause from stage-1 dead money or a top.
- **Climax/topping diagnostics:** parabolic blow-off ENDS the trend (Stryker +65% in 11wk → top); 200-day flattens/rolls over with price whipsawing ACROSS it = Stage-3; Stage-4 confirmed only when the 200-day is in a definite downtrend with price below it.
- **Emerging-leader pulse off a low: advance 15–20%, rest with only a 5–10% pullback**; cohort must hold, not crash back — the rhythm validates the bottom.

## VCP / Setup

- **VCP contractions: each ~50% of the prior** (e.g. 25→10→5%), **2–4 (occ. 5–6) Ts** — shrinking swings track supply absorption.
- **Footprint notation "40W 31/3 4T"** = Weeks / largest-correction% / final-pullback% / T-count — three scalars that carry the supply/demand info.
- **Pivot volume: below the 50-day average, 1–2 days near the base's LOWEST** — a volume vacuum proves sellers have stopped coming.
- **Up-volume must dwarf down-volume** (surges several hundred to ~1,000%); a demand day followed by a bigger down-volume day disqualifies — accumulation can't hide its asymmetric footprint.
- **Base depth by duration:** 3-week base ≤~25%; typical base ≤25–35%; ~1-year base up to 50%; **>60% = redline (fails)**; avoid >2–3x the market's decline — time absorbs supply, so deep-but-long is tolerable, deep-and-fast is failure.
- **Flat base: correction ≤10–15%, buy above the base HIGH** — held within 10–15% proves sellers never gained control; the high is the clean trigger.
- **Base count: 1–2 = prime; 3 = tradable but obvious; 4–5 = late, abrupt-failure-prone** — count proxies how crowded the accumulation has become.
- **3C / cup-completion-cheat qualifiers:** prior move +25–100% (occ. 200–300%) over 3–36 months; above a rising 200-day; pattern 3–45 weeks (most 7–25); correction 15–40% (up to 50% in weak markets), **>60% fails**; cheat plateau itself within 5–10% high-to-low — the earliest legitimate cup buy.
- **Power play / high tight flag:** +100% in <8 weeks on huge volume → tight sideways range correcting ≤20–25% over 3–6 weeks (some in 12 days), final tightness <10% — the only setup bought without fundamentals; VCP digestion still mandatory.

## Fundamentals × Quality

- **Current-quarter EPS (most recent 1–3 q):** 20–25% YoY floor / 30–40%+ for real superperformers / 40–100%+ in a bull market — keeps you in the population institutions are forced to buy; raise the bar when high-growth names are abundant.
- **Sales: triple-digit for emerging leaders** (recent 2–3+ quarters) — revenue is the un-fakeable substrate; EPS without sales is gimmickry. (Home Depot 104/158/191/220% before +698%.)
- **Code 33: 3 consecutive quarters of acceleration in EPS + sales + net margin, simultaneously** — all three inflecting together is multiplicative and filters out cost-cut-only "growth."
- **5% estimate-revision tripwire:** UP ≥5% → outperform, DOWN ≥5% → underperform; watch current quarter AND current/next fiscal year vs 30 days earlier — the threshold where revisions move fund models rather than being noise.
- **Turnaround gate: recent 1–2 quarters +100%+, TTM-EPS recovering toward the old peak, not cost-cut-only** — easy comps make triple-digit the minimum bar that distinguishes a real inflection.
- **Quality is QUALITATIVE — NO numeric ROE / debt / float gate exists.** Read structurally instead:
  - Pricing-power + capital-intensity = structural disqualifier (airline archetype).
  - Margin-decline cause: cost-driven survivable / price-driven terminal.
  - Strip non-recurring GAINS before believing any growth number (can flip +25% → −7%).
- **Inventory:** finished-goods rising much faster than raw/WIP = bearish (made more than it can sell); raw-materials buildup = bullish demand bet. Read the sub-segment, not the total.
- **Double-whammy:** receivables AND inventory both growing ≥2x sales = high-probability earnings hit.
- **Superperformers' age:** typically public 8–10 years before the move; biggest growth in the first 5–10 years post-IPO (80% of 1990s tech winners IPO'd within the prior 8yr).
- **No buy before the primary base** — the IPO pop and the post-IPO selloff are not the buy point; the first proper consolidation emerging to a new high is.

## Leadership & Catalysts

- **Leadership window: names breaking to NEW HIGHS within 4–8 weeks off a market low ARE the leaders** — new highs against thick bear overhead require overwhelming demand.
- **>60% of superperformers ride a group advance** — a lone strong stock in a dead group is the lower-probability bet.
- **Same-store-sales: ~10% healthy floor; 25–30%+ = unsustainable sell-tell** — comps have only price and volume as drivers, both capped; the upper bound is the actionable number.
- **Store rollout: >~100 net new/yr = saturation alarm** — pace acceleration front-loads earnings and precedes the comp collapse by quarters.
- **Prior-cycle leaders:** ~1/3 round-trip (avg subsequent decline 50–70%, 80–90% post-bubble); <25% of one cycle's leaders lead the next — abandon them as candidates.
- **Catalyst test:** must be still-undiscounted AND confirmed in BOTH tape and numbers; deregulation/sector change is its own class (map the second-order group cascade); screen the top 2–3 names (a #2 can take the RS baton), not only #1.

## Risk-as-setup-quality (diagnostic only — no stops/sizing)

- **Healthy-leader correction band: 25–35% healthy / >50% generally fails / 60% redline / >2–3x-market = avoid** — depth proxies trapped overhead supply.
- **Value-trap flag:** absolute 3–5x P/E near a 52-week low + deteriorating fundamentals = leading indicator of an earnings collapse, not a value floor (a 3x multiple = "this E is going to zero").
- **Even quality blue chips can correct 70–99% and take 11–24 years to break even** — company survival ≠ capital survival; the why-avoid-Stage-4 diagnostic.
- **Valuation upgrade after a major price break = short tell**, not a bottom — the upgrade reasons from a stale model the tape has already repriced.

## Execution / Convergence (diagnostic only)

- **Trade only at four-way convergence** (fundamentals + price + volume + regime) — 3-of-4 is a no-trade, not a smaller trade.
- **Largest decline since Stage 2 began = sell signal, even on a great beat** — magnitude relative to the stock's own move is the institutional-exit footprint.
- **Tennis ball vs egg:** healthy pullbacks snap back to new highs within 1–2 weeks max, volume contracting on the dip / expanding on the recovery; widening two-way swings = egg.
- **Two-quarter rolling average over the past 4/6/8 quarters** — smooths noisy single prints into the second-derivative trend.
- **Run several small ANDed screens** and take cross-list recurrence — a monolithic AND-screen has a multiplicative false-rejection rate.
